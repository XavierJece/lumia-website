# **Project Brief: Institutional Website - Lumia Environmental Consulting and Engineering**

## **1. OVERVIEW AND OBJECTIVES**

**Client:** Lumia ‚Äî Environmental Consulting and Engineering
**Main Objective:** Create a modern institutional website based on an APPLE IOS layout, optimized for conversion and user experience, which presents the company as an authority in the environmental sector.

**Business Goals:**

## **2. PROJECT SCOPE - MAIN PAGES**

### **PAGE 01: HOME (Main Landing Page)**

- **Hero Section:** Logo, slogan, primary CTAs ("Hire Consulting" ‚Üí WhatsApp) and secondary ("View Portfolio").
- **Content Sections:**
  1.  Featured Services (cards with icons).
  2.  Commercial Differentiators (Customer Service, Payment, Simplicity).
  3.  Success Cases & Client Logos.
  4.  Impact Metrics (e.g., completed projects, clients served).
  5.  Testimonials (Optional, but desirable).
  6.  FAQ (General frequently asked questions).
- **Footer:** Institutional links, social media, quick contacts, and link to privacy policy.

### **PAGE 02: ABOUT LUMIA**

- History, Mission, Vision and Values.
- Team of Experts Section (photos/short bios).
- In-depth Operational Differentiators.
- Certifications and Associations (with logos).
- Strong visual identity, aligned with the brand manual.

### **PAGE 03: BLOG**

- **Article Listing:** Grid layout, with filters by category (e.g., Licensing, Regularization, PGRS).
- **Internal Article Page:**
  - Title, author, publication date.
  - Body with rich text (HTML), support for images, embedded videos, and graphics.
  - SEO-optimized meta tags (title, description).
  - Sharing buttons for WhatsApp, LinkedIn, Twitter.
